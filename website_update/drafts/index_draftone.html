About Dan's Music Studio

Who We Are

At Dan's Music Studio, we provide a warm, bright, clean, and welcoming environment for the instruction of music. All of our instructors are also working professional musicians who understand what it takes to perform at the highest level of excellence.

Read Our Mission Statement

What We Offer

We offer lessons on most instruments and vocals to students of all ages. Piano guitar, vocal, and drum lessons are quite popular. Lessons are avaliable on most band and orchestral instruments and help to strengthen the skills of students involved in scholastic music programs.
